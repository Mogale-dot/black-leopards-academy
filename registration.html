Terms and Conditions

1. Registration Commitment

Once this contract is signed, a player remains registered to the Academy for one (1) year. If you choose to cancel the agreement, you must notify the Academy within 7 days of signature.

2. Exclusivity

Players are not permitted to contact any other Academies or Clubs during the initial 7-day period, nor throughout the agreed registration period.

3. Behavior Standards

The player's registration means that the player accepts the responsibility of maintaining standards of behavior set out by our Academy.

4. Communication

It is compulsory that the parent/player responds to any communication through the S2S program. Deadline dates for confirmation of attendance will be communicated through the players Head Coach.

5. Payment Terms

The amount indicated (R500.00) will be deducted monthly during the course of this contractual agreement. Should your Debit Order return unpaid, the full amount plus Interest of 2% will be due each time.

6. Attendance Requirements

Should any player attend less than 70% of the training sessions, such player will immediately be expelled from the Academy, and the parent will be liable for the allocated cancellation penalty fee.

7. Release Policy

There is a premature release penalty applicable when players request to be released before the expiration of their contract. The release penalty will be calculated based on remaining contract period.

8. Medical Consent

I consent to my child receiving any medical treatment that the Football Coach reasonably considers necessary or desirable during participation in the Programme.
