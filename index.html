26 May 2025

Coach Thulani on the season ahead.

Known for his discipline and strategic mindset, Coach Thulane is using the off-season to reassess tactics and improve team fitness. “This is the time to reflect, regroup, and rebuild,” he said. “Next season starts with the new project.” His vision is clear: build a more cohesive squad and continue developing players both on and off the field to ensure the Leopards return even hungrier and more prepared.

Young goalkeeper Kelello had great campaign

Spider is making waves with his dedication and performance, under th eyes of the first team. Throughout the past season, Kelello has shown tremendous growth — putting in extra hours on the training ground, improving his reflexes, and building strong communication with the backline.
